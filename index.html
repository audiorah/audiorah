AudioRah, born in India under the name Rahul Patel was raised in New Jersey where he discovered his love for music. His main musical inspirations come from such artists as Diddy, Jay-Z, and the Notorious B.I.G. Even though his musical inspirations descend from a hip hop background, he is also heavily influenced by the sounds of international and dance genres as well. His love for music was later transformed into a talented gift where he is able to blend unthinkable genres of music to create a magnificent symphony to entertain the masses.

He strives to use music as his instrumental tool of influence for individuals and hopefully give them a positive outlook on life. So far he has been able to extend his positive influence up and down the east coast. He has established his reputation in major cities by playing at venues in New York City, Washington D.C., Miami, and Puerto Rico.
